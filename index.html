    Welcome to OpenSafeMap

    This site is designed to provide guidance on adding points to OpenSafeMap in order to capture and record incidents of harrassment. This initiative is inspired by International Womens Day and the high volume of harrassment experienced recently by women on the island of Bali. In honour of this day and in collaboration with the Humanitarian OpenStreetMap Team, a uMap instance powered by OpenStreetMap has been set up to capture incidents of 'Stalking', 'Verbal' and 'Physical' abuse. OpenSafeMap will be reviewed for the inclusion of other types of incidents such as 'Theft', depending on the tools reception and its effectiveness.
